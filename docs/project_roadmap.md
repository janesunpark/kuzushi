## Long-Term Questions

Long-term questions for Kuzushi include both observation-focused questions and project-development questions. 

Observation-focused questions include:

* How do learner-specific problem-solving pathways develop over them?
* How do learners use familiar strategies when task demands increase? 
* When do familiarity support flexibility, and when does it become limiting?
* How do regulation and co-regulation patterns affect task persistence and recovery after difficulty?
* Do similar problem-solving patterns appear across academic, spatial, game-based, and embodied contexts? 

Project-development questions include:

* How can qualitative observation logs be responsibly converted into derived, anonymized data structures?
* Which observation categories are most useful for exploratory visualization and trend analysis?
* How can visualization preserve nuance without turning learners into scores or rankings?
* What kinds of portfolio artifacts best communicate the project's methodology, ethics, and exploratory findings?
